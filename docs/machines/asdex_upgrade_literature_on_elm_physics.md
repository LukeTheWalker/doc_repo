# Literature on ELM Physics

## Experimental work

### ELM control

- V. Igochine (ed.), Active Control of Magneto-hydrodynamic Instabilities in Hot Plasmas (2015) [PDF](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-662-44222-7)

### Measurements of pedestal profile evolutions during ELMs

- F. Laggner, Inter-ELM pedestal structure development in ASDEX Upgrade, PhD thesis (2017) [PDF](https://pure.mpg.de/view/item_2453008)
- M. Dunne et al, Measurement of neoclassically predicted edge current density at ASDEX Upgrade, Nucl. Fusion 52 (2012) [PDF](https://doi.org/10.1088/0029-5515/52/12/123014)  -- *Including evolution of toroidal plasma current during ELM crash*
- C. Bowman et al, Pedestal evolution physics in low triangularity JET tokamak discharges with ITER-like wall, Nucl. Fusion 58 (2018) [PDF](https://doi.org/10.1088/1741-4326/aa90bc)
- H.R.Wilson, Inter-ELM pedestal evolution in low triangularity JET-ILW discharges, EPS 2017 [abstract](https://kth.diva-portal.org/smash/record.jsf?pid=diva2%3A1324931&dswid=-4171)

### Heat deposition onto divertor targets

- T. Eich et al, papers on divertor heat flux: [2005](http://dx.doi.org/10.1088/0741-3335/47/6/007); [2007](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jnucmat.2007.01.240); [2009](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jnucmat.2009.01.202)

### Detachment / High field side high density (HFSHD)

- [Potzel 2015](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jnucmat.2014.12.008)
- [Reimold 2017](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.nme.2017.01.010)
- F. Reimold, Detachment studies and poloidal scrape-off layer asymmetries in ASDEX Upgrade H-modes, Slides 2017

### Rotation measurements

- E. Viezzer information on rotation in normal field direction in AUG [PDF](http://dx.doi.org/10.1088/0029-5515/53/5/053005) and reversed field direction (reversed B_tor and I_p) [PDF](assets/asdex_upgrade/revbtip.pdf)
- M. Cavedon, The role of the radial electric field in the development of the edge transport barrier in the ASDEX Upgrade tokamak, PhD thesis (2016) [PDF](https://mediatum.ub.tum.de/1295389)
- * M. Cavedon et al, The role of radial electric field and neoclassical transport in the establishment and sustainment of the edge transport barrier in the ASDEX Upgrade tokamak, EPS (2017) [PDF](https://boa.unimib.it/handle/10281/403381)

### QH-Mode

- W. Suttrop et al, Stationary ELM-free H-mode in ASDEX Upgrade, 9th EU-TTF workshop [Slides PDF](https://info.fusion.ciemat.es/ttf2002/contributions/Suttrop.PDF)
- W. Suttrop et al, Study of quiescent H-mode plasmas in ASDEX Upgrade, Plasma Phys. Control. Fusion 46, A151 (2004) [PDF](https://doi.org/10.1088/0741-3335/46/5A/016)
- W. Suttrop et al, Studies of the 'Quiescent H-mode' regime in ASDEX Upgrade and JET, Nucl. Fusion 45, 721 (2005). [PDF](http://dx.doi.org/10.1088/0029-5515/45/7/021) -- *Observation of QH-Mode in ASDEX Upgrade including Edge Harmonic Oscillation*
